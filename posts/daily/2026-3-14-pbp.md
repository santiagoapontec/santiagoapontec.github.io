---
title: Retroactive fix
date: 3/14/2026
---

Daily's consist of two types of content, Play by Play's and Reflections. At the start of this website the idea was for reflections to be the main part of a Daily - they'd be thoughtful pieces about anything and everything I might've been thinking about on a given day.

It was apparent after a couple days of doing these that it was a task challenging enough to make it unsustainable long-term.

That's where Play by Play's came in. PBP's are time-stamped annotations about what it is that I am doing with my day, they're magnitudes lower in friction than reflections and also help with accountability a lot more. Yet, I decided still to keep these gatekept behind the daily reflections - in an attempt to force me to write those still.

That also turned out to be a challenge, and so finally on April 13th 2026 I decided to change the presentation of these two pieces of content to make PBP's always visible, and leaving reflections as optional.

This message then serves both as an explanatory note and also as a retroactive fix so that in day's in which PBP's were not yet in existance the written reflections are still accessible.
