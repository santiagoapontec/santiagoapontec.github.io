Sound is a longitudinal wave that travels through a medium. Its speed varies depending on the medium:

$$v_{sound} \approx 343 \text{ m/s in air}$$

The Doppler effect describes how the observed frequency changes when the source is moving:

$$f' = f \cdot \frac{v + v_o}{v - v_s}$$
