Work is defined as force applied over a distance:

$$W = F \cdot d \cdot \cos(\theta)$$

Kinetic energy is the energy of motion:

$$KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$$

And the work-energy theorem states:

$$W = \Delta KE$$
