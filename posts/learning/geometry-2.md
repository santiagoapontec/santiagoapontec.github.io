Some essential area formulas:

$$\text{Rectangle: } A = l \times w$$

$$\text{Triangle: } A = \frac{1}{2} \times b \times h$$

$$\text{Circle: } A = \pi r^2$$

And the circumference of a circle:

$$C = 2\pi r$$
